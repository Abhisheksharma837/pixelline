Introduction Pixeline is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains how we handle your data and what information we collect. Data Collection We collect QR code data from your notebooks, convert them into PDFs, and temporarily store the data for your use. None of this data is stored on our servers. Data Erasure Once the content is scanned and converted, you can erase it from your notebook and reuse it. Pixeline does not retain any scanned data after the process is complete. Your Rights You have full control over your data, and Pixeline ensures that no personal data is shared with third parties.
