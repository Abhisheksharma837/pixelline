How to Use Pixeline

Unlock the full potential of your Pixeline notebook with these simple steps and start reusing your pages effortlessly:

Step 1: Scan the QR Code

Open the app and scan the QR code on your notebook to link it with Pixeline.

Step 2: Capture Your Notes

Use the camera feature to capture the content of your notebook, and the app will convert it into a PDF.

Step 3: Erase and Reuse

Erase the notebook content after saving the PDF. Your notebook is now ready for reuse!

Tips:

  • Ensure the QR code is clear and visible for accurate scanning.
  • Use a well-lit environment to capture high-quality images.
